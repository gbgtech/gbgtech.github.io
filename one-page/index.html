ABOUT

#GBGTECH is an umbrella for different events for entrepreneurs, techies, consultants and investors in Gothenburg. During tonight's launch of the concept, we will talk about what the meetings in the future will contain. Together we'll create content for the future of our Gothenburg Startup community!

Ignited by Gothenburg Startup Hack and curated by Startup Ping Pong, Startup Grind Gothenburg and HUB Gothenburg.

Are you the Ping Pong Champion at your office? Or do you just want to meet other entrepreneurs and get inspired of the competitive ambience? No matter what, this will be a great opportunity to make new friends, have a beer with some familiar faces, as well as battle in a game of ping pong.

The event is totally free and anyone is allowed to watch.

The bartenders are ready and you don’t need your own racket. Just come as you are! Though, if you want to bring a racket and sweatbands – go for it!
