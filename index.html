Gothenburg's Tech and Startup Community

What is #gbgtech?

#gbgtech wants to position Gothenburg as an attractive tech- and startup-commuity in Scandinavia.

The purpose of #gbgtech is two-folded:

  1. Adding light to the tech- and startup scene in Gothenburg
    1. Locally, nationally, internationally
    2. Draw business and investment attention
  2. Being a portal
    1. Entry gate to participate in the tech- & startup scene in Gothenburg

Who is a part of #gbgtech?

  1. You who consider yourself to be within
    1. Tech
    2. Startup
    3. Or Tech- and Startup

  2. You who believe in empowering the tech- and startup-community of Gothenburg

  3. You who are curious about the tech- and startup-community of Gothenburg
